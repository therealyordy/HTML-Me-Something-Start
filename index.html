Home

Home feels right. My home is Kansas City, Missouri. Have you been? It's the best sport city there is right now! Go Chiefs! I moved to Kansas City when I was 7 years old. About Me

I was born in Cuba. Yes, the small little country 90 miles south of Florida. I lived there till I was 7 years old. So I flew across country early in life. Which is why I am probably so adventures. It was hard to learn a new language, but after some years I was capable of speaking fluent. Many years later in my teens, I started to travel to other states and became inlove with going on trips with friends. Never did I think I would have the opputunity to work from home and travel the United States.

Locations
